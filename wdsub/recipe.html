Recipe For Chaos

  1. Preperation

    • Have a friend you aren't afraid to lose
    • Make sure friend has kids
    • Have friend's phone number
    • Buy Doughnuts of all diffrent kinds
    • Get noisy toys (like toy kazoo)
    • Start Driving to Friend's House

  2. Text Friend

    1. Text your Friend "I'm here to bring the chaos"

  3. Enter House

    1. Get out of Car
    2. Grab Doughnuts and Noisy Toys
    3. Close Car Door
    4. Lock Car
    5. Knock on front door

  4. Talk to the Minions

    1. Say:
      • "I got a little treat for ya'll"
      • "Your Mom Said ya'll can stay up all night"
      • "Let's all go and scream for an hour"
    2. Give out your collected items (Kazoo and Doughnuts)

  5. Say Goodbye (To the minions and your friendship)

    1. Exit the house before your friend kills you or keys your car
    2. Get into Car
    3. Drive
    4. Block Friend
    5. (Optional) Change your identity

  6. Have Fun Knowing You Are The Choas!
